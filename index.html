About Me

From Taipei to San Francisco, from Investment Banking to Online Florist, I will describe myself as a truly diverse individual. Born and raised in Taiwan, I attended an international highschool in Asia. I speak English, Mandarin, Taiwanese, and Spanish. I moved to the United States 4 years ago to attend UC Berkeley, where I majored in Economics and interned at various investment banks. However, after I graduated, I decided to start Bovi Flowers, an online luxury floral gift shop that specializes in "real roses that lasts over 1 year." Now I am ready to become a full stack coder and rock the world with some cool websites!

On a more personal note, I love dogs, hate cats. I enjoy cuisines from all over the world, no matter how disgusting they look or smell (stinky tofu from Taiwan, yum). San Francisco is like a second home to me, I find it vigorous yet relaxing. Although I miss home quit a bit, I believe that San Francisco will more and more become my home for the future to come.
